About me

Hi! I'm Leandro Soto Miranda, a Computer Engineer specialized in data science and technology. Throughout my career, I have developed solid technical skills in Python, SQL, machine learning, y cloud computing, with a specialization in implementing Azure and GCP based solutions.

I have had the opportunity to work in a variety of roles, including Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Data Analyst, and Software Engineer. In each of these roles, I have been able to apply my knowledge to develop efficient and results-oriented solutions.

Some of the projects that stand out the most include automating through OCR and fuzzy logic the capture of images and extract information from inventory sheets, which significantly improved the customer experience, and an extensive data analysis on earthquakes in Chile, where I used advanced analysis tools and data visualization to identify patterns and trends.

I am constantly learning, having completed certifications such as IBM's Python 101 for Data Science and Machine Learning from A to Z at Udemy. Currently, I am expanding my skills in project development with LangChain and other software development technologies.

My near-term goals include continuing to develop my skills in key areas of data science and artificial intelligence, and applying this knowledge to solve complex problems and create value in any team I work on.

When I am not immersed in the data world, I enjoy exploring new technologies, and I am passionate about continually learning to improve both personally and professionally.
